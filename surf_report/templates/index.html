    San Juan Forecast

    Some surf on tap this week. We will see a warm front approach our area from the south Tuesday, then a weak low will cross our coastal waters Wednesday. This will generate onshore flow with E winds and a chunky 4-5′ SE swell. Sloppy conditions will continue into Thursday with winds shifting more southerly, but a cold front will pass late Thursday night giving us offshore winds Friday morning. Unfortunately the swell will be declining in size, but still some really fun 3-4′ combo swell with clean conditions all day so get out there! Small leftovers Saturday. Have fun!
